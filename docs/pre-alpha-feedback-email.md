# Pre-Alpha Week 1 Feedback Survey Email Template

This is a manual template that can be sent to testers approximately a week after they accept their invitation.

---

**Subject:** How is Orb working for you? (Pre-Alpha feedback)

Hi [name],

It has been a week since you started using Orb, and I hope you've had a chance to try it out!

Our main goal during this pre-alpha is to understand if Orb's **ambient workload reflection** (the glowing Orb) and **strategic AI features** provide genuine value compared to traditional to-do lists.

If you have a quick 3 minutes, I would appreciate your thoughts on these three questions:

1. **The Ambient Orb:** Does the ambient Orb (its color shifts, pulse rates, and solar flares) actually help you stay aware of your workload without constantly checking lists, or do you ignore it?
2. **Strategic Guidance:** Has the Orb's strategic support—such as asking *"What should I do next?"* or receiving proactive summaries—provided genuine value in organizing your day compared to traditional todo lists?
3. **Friction & Bugs:** Did you run into any major friction points or confusing behavior when talking to the Orb, switching between views (List, Checklist, Kanban), or navigating settings?

You can reply directly to this email with your answers. Alternatively, next time you open the app, the Orb might ask you these questions directly in our conversation window if you prefer to answer there.

Thank you again for testing and helping me build this!

— Stan
