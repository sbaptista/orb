# Pre-Alpha Success Criteria

**Cohort:** 2 testers, tech-savvy, fully isolated workspaces.
**Timeline:** Wave 1 invite May 18 (target), May 25 (hard deadline).

## Success Signals (within 7 days of invite)

1. Both testers log in and complete the welcome flow
2. At least one tester creates a task using the conversational interface without Stan walking them through it
3. At least one piece of feedback is filed via the Orb Feedback project

## Transition to Alpha

Pre-alpha ends and transitions to alpha when:
- All blocker bugs reported by testers are resolved
- At least one tester has been using Orb for real tasks (not just testing)

## Week 1 Retro (May 25)

Review:
- What broke
- What got used
- What was ignored
- Plan next iteration
